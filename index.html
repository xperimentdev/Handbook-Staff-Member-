STAFF RULES

🟩 Professionalism

Always remain respectful and professional when speaking to members. Avoid slang such as u, ik, omg, ong, etc.

⚖ Follow Server Rules

Being staff does not give permission to break server rules. Lead by example.

🔨 No Abuse of Power

Never mute, kick, timeout, or ban someone without a valid reason and evidence.

👥 Stay Neutral

Listen to both sides before making a decision. Never choose sides because of friendships.

🛡 Respect Staff

Respect decisions made by higher staff and cooperate with the moderation team.

🔒 Confidentiality

Never leak staff chats, private reports, screenshots, or internal discussions.

⭐ Activity

Remain active whenever possible by helping members with tickets, reports, and questions.

📋 Proper Punishments

Follow the punishment ladder and always collect evidence before punishing users.

🤝 No Favoritism

Friends and regular members must always be treated equally.

❓ Ask When Unsure

If you are uncertain about a situation, ask higher staff before acting.

🚫 No Power Tripping

• Don't bait players into breaking rules.
• Don't use your staff rank to threaten players.
• If you're biased or frustrated, allow another moderator to handle the situation.
